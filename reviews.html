Gordon Ramsay

Born 8 November 1966

Gordon Ramsay is a famous British chef, television personality, and writer. Gordon Ramsay is known on TV for his fiery temper and frequent use of profanity.

World famous Chef and TV star Gordon Ramsay visited our establishment not too recently and he had this to say:

"When I first came to this establishment I thought I was in for it...thank f***k I was wrong because this restaurant should be your number 1 choice if you're within 500 miles of it."

Guy Fieri

Born 22 January 1968

Guy Fieri is a famous American chef, television personality, and writer. He owns a multitude of restaurants across California and Las Vegas and is known for hosting TV shows on the Food Network.

World famous Chef and TV star Guy Fieri visited The Kitchen on a warm summer's day. He was wearing his trademark flames shirt and after some locals suggested our restaurant, he came to inspect.

On his visit, Fieri shared laughs and took photos with the staff before taking his seat for dinner.

Needless to say, here at The Kitchen, we take pride in our grilling abilities and lived up to his high expepectations, securing his positive review.
